The Love Story of Valentine & Flowers

The tradition of giving flowers on Valentine's Day dates back to ancient Rome, where Saint Valentine defied Emperor Claudius II by secretly performing marriages for young lovers. Legend has it that before his execution, Valentine sent the first "valentine" greeting to his beloved, signing it "From your Valentine" - a phrase still used today.

Flowers, especially roses, became symbols of this eternal love story. The red rose, gifted by lovers throughout history, represents deep passion and devotion. At Ay's Valentine Lover's Concept, we honor this timeless tradition by ensuring every bouquet carries the same spirit of romance, sacrifice, and undying love that Valentine himself embodied.

But love isn't confined to just one day! We believe that every day is an opportunity to celebrate love - whether it's romantic love, family bonds, friendship, or self-love. From Lagos to every corner of Western Nigeria, we deliver fresh blooms that speak the universal language of affection.

Our Vision

To keep the spirit of Valentine alive every single day, making Western Nigeria the region where love blooms eternally through the beauty of fresh flowers and heartfelt gestures.

Our Mission

To honor Saint Valentine's legacy by helping you express love in its purest form - through the timeless gift of flowers. We craft each bouquet with the same passion and dedication that inspired the first valentines centuries ago.
